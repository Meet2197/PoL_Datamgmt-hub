PoL Data Management Tools

These are data management tools available to researchers PoL.

These tools support the complete research data lifecycle from experiment planning to publication and long-term preservation.

eLabFTW :

  • an open source electronic lab notebook for researchers.
  • an ELN manager for research teams that, lets you store and organize your research experiments easily.

https://elab.cmcb.tu-dresden.de/dashboard.php
