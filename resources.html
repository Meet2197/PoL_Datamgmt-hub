OwnCloud TUD

OwnCloud at TU Dresden provides a secure and compliant cloud storage solution, allowing researchers to store, share, and collaborate on their data with ease. It's an excellent choice for internal data management and collaboration within the university network.

Link

Zenodo

Zenodo is a general-purpose open-access repository developed by CERN. It allows researchers to deposit research data, software, publications, and more. It provides a persistent identifier (DOI) for each upload, ensuring your research output is citable and long-lasting.

Link

Figshare

Figshare is an online repository for researchers to publish their data, figures, and other research outputs. It supports all file types and provides persistent, citable DOIs. Figshare is known for its user-friendly interface and focus on making research accessible and shareable.
