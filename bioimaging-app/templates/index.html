Data Management Tools for PoL TU Dresden

This repository provides information about essential data management tools available to researchers at the Professorship of Laser Technology (PoL) at TU Dresden. These tools support the complete research data lifecycle from experiment planning to publication and long-term preservation.

Overview

Research data management is crucial for reproducible science and compliance with funding requirements.

eLabFTW

A free and open source electronic lab notebook for researchers. eLabFTW is an electronic lab notebook manager for research teams that lets you store and organize your research experiments easily.

Open Source Multi-team Support Database Integration
Access eLabFTW

OMERO

Open Microscopy Environment Remote Objects (OMERO) is currently the most widely used and best-established bioimage data platform for managing and maintaining high-end instruments.

Image Management Multi-format Support Web Interface
Access OMERO

Bio-image Archive

The BioImage Archive will ensure reproducibility of published studies that derive results from image data and reduce duplication of effort, helping scientists generate new insights through reuse of existing data.

Data Repository Reproducibility Public Access
Access Archive

bioimage.io

BioImage Model Zoo - A community-driven, open resource for sharing standardized AI models for bioimage analysis with integrated AI chatbot support.
